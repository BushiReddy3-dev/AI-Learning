# Day 4 Notes - Generative AI

## What is Generative AI?

Generative AI creates new content such as text, code, images, and audio.

## What is an LLM?

Large Language Model trained on huge text datasets.

Examples:

- GPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Llama

## What are Tokens?

Small units of text processed by LLMs.

## What are Embeddings?

Numerical representations of text used for similarity search.

## What is Prompt Engineering?

Writing effective prompts to obtain better responses.

## What is RAG?

Retrieval Augmented Generation combines vector search with LLMs.

## What are AI Agents?

Systems that reason and perform actions using tools.

## Key Learning

Generative AI systems are built using LLMs, embeddings, prompts, vector databases, and retrieval mechanisms.
