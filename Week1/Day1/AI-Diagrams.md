## Diagram Explanation

This diagram illustrates the relationship between Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Generative AI.

### AI
The broad field of creating intelligent systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence.

### Machine Learning
A subset of AI that learns patterns from data.

- Supervised Learning
- Unsupervised Learning
- Reinforcement Learning

### Deep Learning
A subset of Machine Learning based on neural networks.

- CNN
- RNN
- Transformers

### Generative AI
Creates new content such as text, images, audio and code.

- LLMs
- Image Models
- Audio Models
- AI Agents
